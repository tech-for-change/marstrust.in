Our Vision

A compassionate society where animals' right to live is treated on par with that of humans.

Our Mission

To feed, vaccinate, and spay/neuter stray animals; to help injured animals and to fight animal cruelty.

Our Values

To be compassionate towards animals and people at all times; to seek innovative ways to promote and improve the well-being of animals.

Our Long-Term Goals

To continue the good work we do across cities, and in due course across states; to conduct awareness programs in schools, colleges etc.

Get Involved

Even with the number of volunteers we have, sometimes the number of reported incidents becomes too many for us to handle. Little help you do makes a huge difference. We at MARS consider our volunteers to be our biggest asset.

Our volunteers may be asked to work in the following areas:

  • Reporting a injured or sick animal
  • Reporting animal abuse
  • Taking a injured or sick animal in your area to a nearby vet
  • Helping us in feeding stray animals and those in pounds
  • Fostering
  • Social Media Management
  • Seeking Sponsors

What We Do

MARS was formed with the idea that every animal deserves a chance at life. We tirelessly strive towards making this world a better place for animals.

Feeding Stray Animals

Our everyday tasks include feeding stray animals with the help of our sponsors.

Vaccinating Strays

Without vaccination, many stray animals die of preventable diseases.

Spaying/Neutering Strays

We spay/neuter strays to help keep their population in check.

Helping Injured and Sick Animals

We provide medical treatment to injured and sick animals found on the streets.

Fostering and Adoption

Since disabled and abandoned animals can't be left on the street, we foster and try to find a home for them.

Fighting Animal Cruelty

We do not tolerate animal abuse; we lawfully fight animal cruelty of all sorts.

Report An Incident

Give voice to the voiceless. Report animal related incidents.
