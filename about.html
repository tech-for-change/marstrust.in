Who We Are

We were just a couple of individuals doing what we can to help animals and by chance, we crossed paths. We decided to form a group so that we can achieve more by focusing our efforts into a more structured plan for our cause. We have been working together ever since and everyday, we tirelessly strive towards making this world a better place for animals.

What Motivates Us

When there is a chance to prevent suffering, we believe it’s one’s duty to act, not a choice. While it’s immensely fulfilling to see wagging tails and hear happy purrs, to behold heartwarming gratitude expressed by animals, it’s not about what we get back, but how we can make a difference. Just witnessing animals fighting bravely to survive is remarkably motivating and that’s what keeps us going.
